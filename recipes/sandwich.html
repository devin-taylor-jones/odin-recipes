Sandwich Recipe

Description

This is classic bachelor food that's cheap and easy.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Deli sliced Turkey Breast
  • Deli sliced American Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Condiments

Steps:

  1. Grab two pieces bread and place open face on plate
  2. Bunch up deli meat and play upon bread
  3. Layer the lettuce on top of the deli meat
  4. On opposite bread, spread desired condiments
  5. Place cheese on top of the condiments
  6. Fold the two bread slices together
